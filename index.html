Koreaan Japchae

  • Koreaan Japchae
  • Orange Marmelade
  • Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • Sweet potato noodles
  • Beef short ribs
  • Onio
  • Carrots
  • Bell pepper
  • Shiitake
  • Baby spinach
  • Green onions
  • Salt and pepper
  • Garlic
  • Sesale oil
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sugar

How to make:

  1. Cutting the beef ribs:
    • Trim excess fat.
    • Cut the meat of the bone.
    • Cut in half horizontally.
    • Cut strips.
  2. Cooking the Japchae:
    • Marinate beef for about 20 minutes (with soy sauce, sugar, pepper, garlic).
    • Mix the sauce ingredients (soy sauce, sesame oil, sugat, salt and pepper, garlic) in a very large bowl.
    • Cook the noodles per pack directions.
    • Rinse noodles briefly under tap water, shake off excess water.
    • Place noodles into the sauce, do not mix.
    • Cook the onion, shiitake, and white part of spring onions.
    • Add the carrot and bell pepper and cook to a "tender crisp".
    • Add spinach and the green part of spring onions, toss for about a minute.
    • Add veggies to the noodle bowl.
    • Cook the beef, high heat, until no more red.
    • Add beef to the noodle bowl.
    • Add sesame seeds.
    • Toss until the sauce is evenly distributed.
  3. Done!
