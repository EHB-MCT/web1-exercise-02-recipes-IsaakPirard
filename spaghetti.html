Spaghetti

  • Koreaanse Japchae

  • Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Ground meat
  • Tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Dried oregano
  • Red pepper flakes

How to make:

  1. Making meat sauce:
    • Brown the ground meat in a bit of oil
    • Stir in onions (until they soften).
    • Add garlic, tomato paste, oregano and red pepper flakes.
    • Add water (or broth) and crushed tomatoes.
    • Simmer for about 25 minutes.
  2. Cooking the pasta:
    • Boil some water in a pot/kettle. It has to be enough to completely submerge your spaghetti.
    • Add salt into the pot. I suggest about a tablespoon per pound of pasta.
    • Cook the pasta for as long as the packaging suggests.
    • After the spaghetti is cooked, dispose of the water and toss the pasta into your sauce.
  3. Serve with some parmesan cheese on top.
  4. Enjoy!
